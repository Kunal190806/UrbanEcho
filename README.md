# UrbanEcho: Your Smart City Companion

This is a Next.js starter project created in Firebase Studio.

UrbanEcho is an AI-powered urban companion designed to make city living more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable. It acts as a local friend, providing real-time information, personalized suggestions, and essential services to help you navigate and discover your city.

## Core Features

### Energy Hub (Home)
The central feature of UrbanEcho, providing a comprehensive dashboard for managing home energy consumption.
- **AI Energy Optimization Engine**: Predicts next-day energy demand by analyzing usage patterns, weather, and tariffs. Recommends optimal times to run high-power appliances to minimize costs.
- **Smart Appliance Integration (IoT Simulation)**: View live status, power consumption, and efficiency levels of connected smart devices. Remotely toggle schedules to save energy.
- **Community & Behavior Change Tools**: Earn "EcoPoints" by completing daily energy-saving challenges. Track your impact with CO₂ savings and an eco-score on a weekly leaderboard.
- **Weekly Usage & Breakdown Charts**: Visualize your energy consumption over the week and see which appliances are using the most energy.
- **AI-Powered Usage Alerts**: Receive personalized alerts and tips based on your real-time usage patterns to reduce consumption.

### City Dashboard
A centralized hub for navigating daily urban life with a collection of smart widgets and tools.
- **Smart Widgets**: A carousel of real-time information including an energy snapshot, city pulse (traffic, air quality), upcoming metro schedules, and nearby EV/petrol stations.
- **Personalized & Eco-Friendly Route Planning**: Plan your journey with preferred modes of transport and get suggestions for routes with the lowest carbon footprint.
- **Discover New Places**: An AI-powered recommender suggests new points of interest based on your travel history and preferences.
- **AI Memory Journal**: Save your favorite places and memories using simple, natural language commands.
- **Safety Hub**: Access an SOS button, emergency helplines, and view local safety alerts.

### City Energy Analytics Dashboard
A powerful tool for urban planners and administrators to monitor collective energy trends.
- **Aggregated City-Wide Data**: View total energy usage, average per-household consumption, and peak usage hours.
- **Advanced Visualizations**: Analyze data with consumption trend graphs, a peak usage heatmap, and a bar chart comparing usage by zone.
- **Efficiency Leaderboard**: Rank neighborhoods or zones by their energy efficiency scores to identify areas for improvement.

### Work & Study Spaces
Discover and connect to nearby work hubs, study cafés, and co-working spaces.
- **Search & Filter**: Find the perfect spot by searching and filtering based on amenities and availability.
- **View Details**: Each listing includes location, amenities, availability status, and user ratings.

### City Library
Explore top attractions and hidden gems in any city.
- **City Search**: Search for attractions in any city by name.
- **Attraction Cards**: View detailed cards for each location, including an image, description, and rating.
- **Save Your Favorites**: Bookmark places you want to visit and view them in your saved list.
