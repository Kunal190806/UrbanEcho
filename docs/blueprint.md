# **App Name**: UrbanEcho: Smart City Navigator

## Core Features:

- Personalized Route Planning: Suggest optimal travel routes using various transport modes based on user preferences and real-time data.
- Favorite Places Memory: Remembers and quickly navigates to frequently visited locations.
- New Place Recommendations: Suggests new points of interest based on user history and AI analysis of city data.
- Multi-Modal Transport Integration: Combines route planning across metro, bus, EV, and auto systems.
- Smart City Integration: Integrates with a smart city services and platforms to gather data.
- Sustainable Route Options: Prioritizes and suggests the routes that minimize carbon footprint, considering the availability of public transportation, EV charging stations, and pedestrian paths, presented using the output from an LLM as a tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#2E8B8E) to evoke trust and connection to urban landscapes.
- Background color: Light grayish-blue (#E0F2F7) for a clean and unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft yellow (#F0E68C) for highlighting points of interest and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a blend of modern feel and readability.
- Use clean, modern icons that represent various transport modes and city landmarks.
- Intuitive map-based layout with easily accessible options and route details.
- Subtle animations for transitions and real-time updates, enhancing user experience.
